[name]
LibGDX

[description]
LibGDX is a cross-platform 2D and 3D game development framework written in
Java/C/C++.<br/><br/>

It's free for commercial and non-commercial use, pretty powerful
and lots of fun to work with! Or at least no-one complained yet.<br/><br/>
Write your game once, deploy to Windows, Linux, Mac OSX and Android!
